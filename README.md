# 🤝 bit.buddy

> An onboarding assistant that helps new hires and managers streamline the onboarding process for a seamless transition.

Feel free to explore our project here: [Website](https://biakuya.github.io/bit-buddy-onboarding/)
---

## 📖 About

**bit.buddy** is a web-based onboarding platform designed to bridge the gap between new employees and their managers. It provides a structured, role-specific experience so that both parties can navigate the onboarding journey with clarity and confidence.

Whether you're a new hire trying to get up to speed or a manager overseeing the transition, bit.buddy gives you the right tools, timelines, documents, and team information — all in one place.

---

## ✨ Features

- 🔐 **Pre-login Landing Page** — A welcoming entry point before users authenticate
- 👤 **Dual Role Support** — Separate, tailored dashboards for **New Hires** and **Managers**
- 📋 **Onboarding Page** — A guided onboarding flow to get new hires started quickly
- 📅 **Timeline Tracker** — View and manage onboarding milestones and deadlines
- 📁 **Document Center** — Access and manage important onboarding documents
- 👥 **Team Directory** — Browse team members and understand your organization
- 📚 **Resources Hub** — Curated resources to support learning and integration
